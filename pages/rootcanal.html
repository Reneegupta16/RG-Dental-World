Root Canal Treatment [RCT]

Root canal treatment, also known as endodontic treatment, is a procedure to repair and save a badly damaged or infected tooth instead of removing it. The treatment involves removing the infected pulp from the tooth, cleaning and disinfecting the root canals, and then filling and sealing them.

At our clinic, we specialize in performing root canal treatments with precision and care. Our experienced endodontists use advanced techniques and equipment to ensure the success of each procedure and the comfort of our patients throughout their treatment journey.

If you're experiencing severe tooth pain, sensitivity to hot or cold, swelling around the gums, or discoloration of the tooth, you may need root canal treatment. Contact us today to schedule a consultation and learn more about how root canal therapy can relieve your pain and preserve your natural smile.
