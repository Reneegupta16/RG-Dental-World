Our Services

At RG Dental World, we offer a wide range of dental services to help you achieve the perfect smile.

  • Smile Makeover: Comprehensive cosmetic procedures to enhance your smile.
  • Root Canal Treatments: Effective solutions to save infected teeth.
  • Laminates and Veneers: Thin coverings to improve the appearance of teeth.
  • Invisible Aligners: Clear, custom-made aligners to straighten teeth without metal braces.
  • Tooth Jewellery: Decorative gems and crystals for your teeth.
  • Tooth Whitening: Professional teeth whitening services for a brighter smile.
  • Complete and Partial Dentures: Removable appliances for missing teeth.
  • Crowns (Zirconia, PFM, Emax, CAVA): Durable and aesthetic crowns to protect and restore teeth.
  • Bridges: Fixed prosthetics to replace one or more missing teeth.
  • Paediatric Dentistry: Specialized care for children's dental needs.
